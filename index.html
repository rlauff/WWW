Robert Lauff

I am a PhD candidate at the Technische Universität Berlin, advised by Stefan Felsner. My research interests include structured graph theory and combinatorics. Recently I have focused on finding constructions for nice cycles and paths on polytopes, such as rainbow cycles or facet-Hamiltonian cycles.
